Side Project Internships4You

App development using PHP, SQL,HTML/CSS

Over the past month, I have been working on a web-application which helps employers and students post/jobs, mainly internships. I created this web-app using PHP, SQL and HTML/CSS.

I decided to create this app after seeing that many students had lost their internships due to covid-19 and were in deseperate need of reaching out to employers who were still hiring for remote interns. I decided to create a platform where students can search for active and newly posted internships while searching through various catergories.I made this app user friendly by eliminating a log in system and allowing job posts to be made faster.
